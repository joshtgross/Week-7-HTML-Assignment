About Us

The New Southern Politico was created to disseminate the news from Capitol Hill to the local citizens of the happenings of State government. Fast forward nearly 180 years later, the NSP is now the leading source of news, politics, and local information for nearly 2 million people regionally.


Our goals for this site:

  • To Provide the News
  • To Storytell
  • Investigate Corruption
